This gauge shows your overall knowledge of all the cards. This is determined from the certainty you've given to each card on the slider below. Move this slider up or down to indicate your level of certainty in knowing the meaning of the current card. Top is "I know it 100%", bottom is "I don't know it at all", anywhere in between is "I know it this much". Press this button to reveal the card's meaning after reciting what you remember of it to yourself. After you've adjusted your certainty score on the slider above, press this button to draw the next card.
What does this card mean? Recite what you remember of the meaning of the displayed card before clicking Reveal.
